Let the wind spin your world around!

Wind turbines convert the wind's kinetic energy to electrical power

The biggest wind turbine that can be found today is called 'MHI Vestas V164 8MW.'

Harnessing power from wind is an ancient practice. Based on wikipedia, "the first known practical windmills were built in Sistan" back in the 7th century. These windmills were used to grind grain or lift water from wells.

Wind turbines have expnentially grown in size and capacity over the years, as the following table shows:
